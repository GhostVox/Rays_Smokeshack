About Ray

Ray’s Smokeshack
Ray’s Smokeshack is more than just a place to grab great barbecue—it’s a dream brought to life. Founded by a dedicated veteran, motorcycle enthusiast, and family man, Ray always had a passion for good food and bringing people together. With a vision of owning his own food truck, he turned that dream into reality, creating a smokeshack where every meal is crafted with care and served with heart. Located in the heart of the city, Ray’s Smokeshack is a family-owned and operated business committed to delivering an exceptional dining experience. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or settling in to enjoy the warm, welcoming atmosphere, you’ll taste the dedication in every bite. Come hungry, leave happy—because at Ray’s, we’re all family.
